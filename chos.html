Caspian Tile

We are a young, but highly competitive company specializing in ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone installation. Available in Ottawa and surrounding areas, we offer affordable pricing along with quality workmanship, creativity, and knowledge of various types of design and patterns. While being fully insured and offering a warranty on all installations, you can rest assured that Caspian Tile will always provide the best service possible for any job, no matter how big or small!

Services

Tile Installation

Shower, Tub surround, Backsplash, Entrance, etc...

Repair

Repair or modification to exisiting tile.

Water Proofing

Installation and warranty of all Schluter products(Ditra Heat, Kerdi, Niches, etc...)

Demolition

Removal of existing tile and preparation
